BTS
방탄소년단 (Bangtan Sonyeondan) aka. Bangtan Boys

TIMELINE
June 11-12,2013
11- their very first Public appearance, Their very first MV "No More Dream" was released. 12 Day their first MV was released, Their first Single Album "2 Cool 4 Skool" was released.
June 13, 2013
BTS had their first Stage Debut performing "No More Dream" at M Countdown and was considered their Debut Date.
July 09, 2013
BTS announced their Fandom's Official Name which is A.R.M.Y.
September 11, 2013
Their First Mini Album "O!RUL8,2?" was released.
November 14, 2013
They won their First Award "Best New Artist" on MMA.
February 11, 2014
They won their Third Award "New Artist of the Year" on the 3rd Gaon Chart K-Pop Awards.
After Winning their First award on Gaon Chart K-Pop Awards, Their Second Mini Album "Skool Luv Affair" was released.
On the same day of release, "Boy In Luv" peaks on Billboard World Albums Chart at 3rd Position, Making it their First Biggest Milestone on Billboard World Albums Chart.
August 19, 2014
BTS First Full Length Album "Dark & Wild" was released.
January 23, 2015
BTS won their First award on 25th Seoul Music Awards as the "New Artist of the Year".
April 29, 2015
Their Eight MV "I Need U" was released and gave them an even more Popularity overseas.
On the same day, BTS Third Mini Albun "The Most Beautiful Moment In Life Pt. 1" was released.
October 26, 2015
BTS Became the Brand Ambassadors for PUMA.
November 30, 2015
"Run" was released, Their Fourth Mini Album "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt. 2" was released.
October 09, 2016
Their Fourteenth MV "Blood Sweat & Tears" was released, with it's extremely unique Aesthetic Theme which made them More Popular.
On the Same day, "Blood Sweat & Tears" Achieves All-Kill, Peaking No. 1 at Melon, MNET, Bugs, olleh, Soribada, Genie, Naver and Monkey 3 Charts. In addition, all 15 tracks from the album have take the top 15 spots on Melon, Genie, MNET, and Naver Music.
October 29, 2016
BTS Hits at First Position on Billboard Social 50 Chart.
On the Same day, BTS hits First Position on World Digital Songs Chart with "WINGS".
November 19, 2016
BTS won their Very first Daesang "Best Album of the Year" at the MMA.
December 02, 2016
BTS won their Second Daesang "Artist of the Year" at the MNET Asian Music Awards, Another Huge Milestone for them.
May 21, 2017
BTS won as "Top Social Artist Award" at the Billboard Music Awards.
September 05, 2017
BTS reached 8 Million Twitter followers and Is currently the most followed Korean Artist in Twitter.
September 18, 2017
Their Fifth Mini Album "Love Yourself: HER" was released
On the Same day, Their Seventeenth MV "DNA" was released.
DNA becomes the First K-Pop Group single to hit U.S. iTunes Top 10 at Fourth Position.
DNA's MV becomes the Eleventh most viewed video of all-time in first 24 hours and Becomes the Fastest K-Pop Music Video by a Group to reach 10 Million Views in Youtube.
Oct. 31, 2017
UNICEF Korea launches Love Myself campaign in collaboration with BTS and Big Hit Entertainment.
Nov. 19, 2017
Performs "DNA" at the AMAs, the first time a K-pop group has performed during a major U.S. award show.
May. 20, 2018
First performance of BTS at the Billboard Music Awards. They are nominated and won in the top social artist category for the second year in row.
August 24, 2018
The group release their compilation album Love Yourself: Answer with the lead single Idol. The album becomes the band’s second one to reach number one in the US market.
September 24, 2018
BTS give a speech at the UN headquarters advising young people to join global efforts against discrimination and poverty. The speech goes viral as #BTSxUnitedNations trends worldwide on Twitter.
March 11, 2019
BTS announces the next their album called Map of the Soul: Persona.
April 17, 2019
BTS appear in Time 100’s list of most influential people.
June 1, 2019
BTS sells out London’s Wembley Stadium while also making history as the first Korean act to do so.
